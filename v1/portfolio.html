Corporate Identity

The M Company

The M Company is a tech startup focused on simplifying and optimizing current mobile and desktop devices to get the most productivity and efficiency out of them. Its name comes from the M theory, which looks to tie all quantum theories together in an elegant manner, just like they hope to simplify tech.

Taking into account what they stand for and their name, I stripped the logo to the simplest form possible without losing the striking feeling the company was looking for.

The website follows the brand's core principles as well. The idea was to create a minimal, to the point site so new potential costumers would know exactly what the company offers.

The business cards are straighforward and sleek.



InkDrink

InkDrink is an online art store. Its team asked for a logo that related to their art style and represented their brand, all while keeping it simple and to the point. Illustrating their brand name in a literal sense was the solution, all while keeping the illustration in cool tones to reflect the usual palette used by the artist.





Flexer

The owners of Flexer approached me with the idea of starting a marketing agency. They wanted to provide flexible general services that adapted to their costumers. I noticed the emphasis they put into the word 'flexible' and suggested the name Flexer for their venture. Then I designed their corporate identity for costumers to visualize exactly what the owners had in mind: adaptability.

Web & UI/UX Design

FontDatabase.app

fontdatabase.app is a concept web application designed to help people find alternative fonts. Using an algorithm, it can help you find fonts more (or less) serious, detailed, formal, etc. to the one you input. Users can also help the algorithm grow by suggesting fonts themselves.

Spur

Spur is an app focused on making setting events and reminders easier than ever, implementing speech recognition. The app is straight to the point and so is the website, to lead costumers straight to the product.
