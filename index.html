About

I am a designer, UX researcher, and developer. Over the past 4 years, my focus has been (and is) creating simple-to-use solutions to complex design problems in the form of interfaces, in their adequate medium.

This is usually done with a combination of the three— I research a problem and what users have to say about it, design a solution and its copy, develop it or instruct how to, and ship it.

Besides freelancing, I am the founder of The M Company, my side project which aims to create products to optimize existing technology, and has created products such as the popular FontDatabase and PH-nominated Vanish.


To learn more about how I conduct my work in each area, check my Projects page.
